🏆 Swing Ranking — Key Parameters & Signal Framework Reference

📈 Moving Averages

  • 20-Day SMA — Short-term trend direction
  • 50-Day SMA — Medium-term trend anchor
  • 200-Day SMA — Long-term secular trend
  • VWAP — Intraday/weekly volume-weighted fair value

📉 Volatility & Levels

  • ATR (14-Day) — Average True Range for stop placement
  • Support (20-Day Low) — Demand zone floor
  • Resistance (20-Day High) — Supply zone ceiling
  • Beta — Systematic risk vs. market benchmark

⚡ Momentum Oscillators

  • RSI (14-Day) — Overbought/Oversold gauge
  • MACD Line — Trend momentum crossover signal
  • MACD Signal Line — Trigger for entry/exit timing
  • MACD Histogram — Divergence confirmation

📊 Volume Analytics

  • Volume SMA (20-Day) — Baseline liquidity threshold
  • Volume Breakout — 1.5x spike above average
  • Weekly Volume Trend — Institutional accumulation
  • Volume vs. Price — Confirmation of move validity

🎯 Price Action

  • Daily Change % — Short-term sentiment pulse
  • Weekly Change % — Swing momentum gauge
  • YTD Change % — Relative strength ranking
  • Mean Price Target — Analyst consensus upside

🚦 Bullish / Breakout Identification Guidelines

🟢
Golden Cross Alignment BULLISH Price trading above 20 SMA > 50 SMA > 200 SMA. All three sloping upward indicates strong bullish trend structure.
📐
VWAP Reclaim BULLISH Price crosses back above VWAP after consolidation below. Signals renewed buying interest at volume-weighted fair value.
💥
Volume Breakout Confirmation BREAKOUT Volume spikes 1.5x above 20-day average while price breaks resistance. High conviction institutional participation.
🌊
RSI Momentum Reversal BULLISH RSI bounces off 30 (oversold) and trends toward 50+. Avoid entries when RSI > 70 unless in strong parabolic trend.
🎯
Resistance Break + Retest BREAKOUT Price closes above 20-day resistance on elevated volume. Ideal entry on retest of broken resistance as new support.
📏
ATR-Based Stop Placement RISK MGMT Set stop-loss at 1.5x–2x ATR below entry. Tight ATR = tighter stops; wide ATR = wider stops or smaller position size.
🏢
Beta-Adjusted Sizing RISK MGMT Beta > 1.2 = high volatility, reduce position size. Beta < 0.8 = defensive, suitable for larger allocations in uncertain markets.
🎯
Mean Target Upside Gap CONVICTION Mean analyst target significantly above current price with positive momentum = asymmetric risk/reward setup.
